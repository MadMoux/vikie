The Story

What are VIKIES?

Deep beneath the great Norse mountains, hidden from giants and gods alike, lies Nanoheim — a realm so small that even the ravens of Odin have flown over it without noticing.

Here, the VIKIES were born. Compact, stubborn, and absolutely fearless — the VIKIES spent centuries perfecting the art of war, brewing legendary mead, and arguing about whose beard is the finest.

When the blockchain was discovered etched into an ancient rune stone, the VIKIES saw their chance — to march out of Nanoheim and be known across all realms forever.

"Small warriors. Big legends."
